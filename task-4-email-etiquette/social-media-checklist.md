#### **Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist for College Students**





##### &#x20;✅ *Do’s*



###### 1\. **Think** **before you post** and make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.

###### 

###### 2\. **Use privacy settings** to control who can see your content and personal information.

###### 

###### 3\. **Verify the information you share** to prevent the spread of misinformation and fake news.

###### 

###### 4\. **Maintain a professional image online**, especially if future employers are likely to see your profiles.

###### 

###### 5\. **Be respectful to others online** by using polite language and refraining from offensive comments.





##### ❌ *Don’ts*



1. ###### **Don’t share your personal information**, like your home address, passwords, and financial details.

###### 

###### 2\. **Don’t engage in cyberbullying** or make offensive comments about others.

###### 

###### 3\. **Don’t accept friend requests from unknown individuals**, as their profiles might be fake.

###### 

###### 4\. **Don’t share inappropriate content**, like pictures and other content that can harm your reputation

###### 

###### 5\. **Don’t click on suspicious links and promotions**, as they might be malware and/or scams.



###### 

###### **The above social media Do’s and Don’ts checklist is intended for college students and helps them use social media responsibly and safely, protecting their privacy, reputation, and online safety.**
