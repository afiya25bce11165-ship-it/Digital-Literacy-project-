### **TASK 5: PART B** 



##### ✅***PREVENTION CHECKLIST*** 





* ###### Use **strong and unique passwords** for your email, social media, and academic accounts. Do not use the same password for multiple accounts.

###### 

* ###### Use **two-factor authentication (2FA)** to provide an additional layer of security to your accounts.

###### 

* ###### **Do not click on suspicious links** sent to you via emails, messages, or social media, especially those that promise rewards, discounts, or urgent account verification.

###### 

* ###### **Be cautious while sharing personal information** like your phone number, addresses, or college information on unknown websites or platforms.

###### 

* ###### **Do not share your UPI PIN or OTP** with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or a payment app. Banks do not ask for your PIN or OTP.

###### 

* ###### **Always verify the payment requests** before approving them in the UPI app. Remember, you are actually transferring money by entering your PIN, not receiving money.

###### 

* ###### **Only download applications from trusted sources** like app stores, and avoid downloading unknown applications or cracking software.

###### 

* ###### **Log out from shared or public computers** in libraries or cyber cafes, and avoid saving passwords on those devices.

###### 

* ###### **Immediately report cyber fraud**  to the “National Cyber Crime Portal( cybercrime.gov.in)”or contact the “Cyber Crime Helpline 1930”  for timely help.
